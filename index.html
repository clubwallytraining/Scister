OCR Image Processor with Clickable Text Highlights and Crop Feature

Upload an image and perform OCR. Use the side menu to select specific areas for OCR or to crop the image. After drawing a crop rectangle, click "Confirm Crop" to apply the crop. Recognized words within drawn rectangles will be highlighted in light blue on the image. Click on a highlighted block to assign the concatenated text to one of the input fields (Item Name Short, Item Name Long, UPC, Location, Downstack, Notes). You can edit fields manually as well. Once done, press "Update Table" to add a row to the table. Process multiple images by clearing the image and fields. Finally, press "Save CSV" to download all collected data.
